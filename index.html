CHECK OUR STEPS

Our 6 easy steps

01

Discover and Research

The first step in our process is to learn everything about you and your business and how we can help. We understand how people find your website, what your customers seek, and your competitor's digital marketing strategies

02

Strategy

We offer you 4-5 design choices for your websites. Our designs are carefully chosen to ensure your website converts leads into customers. In the meantime, we allow our clients to begin uploading all the necessary content for the website.

03

Development & Initial Review

Our web development team begins the website creation on our hidden server. Once the initial design is complete, the client is given a sneak peek and begins the feedback process.

04

Finalization & Quality Testing

The website undergoes final revisions based on the client's feedback. Once complete, its saubjected to thorough testing across various browsers, devices, screen size, and functions like contact forms, navigation, hyperlinks and social media integrations

05

Hands-on Training

We walk your team through the website's back-end to familiarize you with all of the powerful tools of your new website. We cover all content management scenarios and best practices on how to solve them, alongside search engine Optimization techniques, to ensure adjustments are handled correctly.

06

Deployment, Payment & Post Launch Support

All website include complementary software updates and bug fixes for a week after launching your brand new website. During this time, we are ready to address any questions and will use our monitoring software to ensure your website is always online. Following the launch of your website, we work with you to create a customized maintenance plan to keep your website in shape.
